Green Thumb Guide

Keep Your Plants Thriving

Six simple habits that make all the difference between a struggling plant and a flourishing one.

Right Sunlight

Match each plant to a spot that fits its natural light needs — bright indirect for most indoor plants, direct sun for cacti and herbs.

Daily

Mindful Watering

Always check the top inch of soil first. Overwatering is the number-one cause of root rot — when in doubt, wait a day.

Weekly

Organic Soil Mix

Use a well-draining mix rich in organic matter. Good soil is the foundation — it holds the right balance of moisture and air.

Seasonal

Stable Temperature

Most houseplants prefer 18–28 °C. Keep them away from cold drafts, ACs, and heaters that cause sudden temperature swings.

All Seasons

Humidity & Misting

Tropical plants love humidity. Mist leaves lightly, use a pebble tray with water, or group plants together to raise local moisture.

As Needed

Timely Fertilising

Feed every 4–6 weeks during the growing season with a balanced liquid fertiliser. Skip fertilising in winter when growth slows.
