Meet the Founder

Mr. Gaurav Chandulal Kapare

Horticulturist & Founder, GreenSpire Solutions

Gaurav holds a Master’s degree in Horticulture from Dr. Balasaheb Sawant Konkan Krishi Vidyapeeth (BSKKV), Dapoli, Ratnagiri. With a strong foundation in plant science and a deep passion for sustainable, eco-friendly gardening, he brings both expertise and creativity to every project. His vision for GreenSpire Solutions is rooted in creating green spaces that are beautiful, functional, and environmentally responsible. From thoughtfully designed gardens to thriving landscapes, every project reflects his commitment to bringing people closer to nature and transforming outdoor spaces into inspiring, living environments.
